About

Who we are

Gadal Technology PLC is a private owned company that works on technology to make easy the daily activities of people and institutions through online marketing. Currently we are a legal entity to work in e-commerce, supply of construction materials, heavy vehicles and machinery for sale and rent at market rates and quantities. In addition, we provide different buildings, warehouses, villa houses, hotel rooms, halls and light vehicles for sale or rent in all regions of the country.

Our company has been playing a prominent role for the past eight years by working together with the construction sector to enhance the capital and technical capabilities of the private sector, which plays a significant role in economic development and social participation.

In order to enable us to repeat the extensive knowledge and skills developed in the field in other professional we have developed a trading website gadalmarket.com in addition to the existing construction machinery rental and sales service, and has prepared and presented communication network on the sale and rental of vehicles and fixed assets for marketing purposes.

Working Areas of our company

  • Supply of Construction Materials,vehicles and machinery for sale or rent at market rates and quantities.
  • Providing for sale or/and rent of buildings, warehouses, houses, Hotel rooms and vehicles for various purposes in all regions of the country.
  • Online marketing and renting of various items through our website Gadal Market
  • designing various problem-solving projects with the help of technology and provide them to the business community at affordable price.

The Visionary Behind Gadal Technology PLC

Mr. Samson Belda

Gadal Technologies is a company that was founded by Mr. Samson Belda, a young and visionary entrepreneur who wanted to transform the outdated business information exchange system in Ethiopia into a modern and digital one. By providing e-commerce, rental and sale of machineries and properties, and consultancy on various aspects of business and investment, Gadal Technologies aims to serve the needs and preferences of different professionals in various fields.

Gadal Technology is currently employing more than ten strong and hard-working permanent employees, but it is working to organize and receive information from young people in different places, creating job opportunities for many young people; In addition to this, how to convert the time and information resources of the youth into money and provide them with skills training that will benefit themselves and their country is being completed.
